Donut Recipe
Ingredients
    For dough
  • 1 c. whole milk
  • 1/4 c. plus 1 tsp. granulated sugar, divided
  • 1 packet (or 2 1/4 tsp.) active dry yeast
  • 4 1/2 c.all-purpose flour, plus more for surface 1/2 tsp. kosher salt
  • 6 tbsp. melted butter 2 large eggs 1/2 tsp. pure vanilla extract
  • Canola or vegetable oil, for frying
